Introduction


Welcome to My Portfolio, my name is Luciana. I'm an Argentinean girl living in Florida for more than 20 years. I came to USA in 2000 and it's been an amazing journey. First learning the language which I always found it fascinating. That helped me to learn fast and easy. Then adapted myself to the culture and diversity of people. I studied Graphic Design in Mendoza, Argentina and I didn't plan to get into Technology, but in 2007 I started working in IT as Quality Assurance Analyst. That job allowed me to develop an interest toward coding and development. Big part of my job was making sure that the code was doing what it was expected and finding any possible issue that the user might found. Soon I discovered that I wanted to be the one that create an experience for the user, not simple making sure that the code worked. There is a world of possibilities to create and having a designer background I am able to freely create and get to the users. I hope you enjoy visiting my website and feel free to contact me for suggestions and any questions you might have. It's a pleasure to receive feedback. I'm excited to share with you my projects and I hope you find them as good as I felt when I created them. Enjoy!
