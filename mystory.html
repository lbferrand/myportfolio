My Story

Looking back at the year 2000 when I landed in this incredible country. Now, my country USA ❤️ . It's been an incredible and open mind ride at the same time, and always grateful to be an american citizen.



Early Years 1990's

After finishing my Bachelor Degree in Graphic Design in 1995 in Argentina, I worked as an Administrative Manager for an Internet provider. That position opened the curiosity in me about software computers. I enjoyed the opportunity to learn about cable modem and internet. Also, I was able to put my skills to work, creating a new administrative system and designed the logo for the company. It was a great beginning and learning about the internet and computer software was exciting.

A few years later I came to United States and I've been living here since then.


Present

This is a dream that came true. I am an American citizen, working for more than a decade in IT and I love every minute. I recently received certifications as Front End Developer, React Development and I would love to continue my education in all new software languages to become proficient in all of them. I'm interested in becoming a Full Stack Developer soon. I am very passionate about coding.


Future

Nobody can predict the future, but we can all work for a better one. So, I am dreaming of having an incredible career as a Developer. Increasing my knowledge to be able to work as Software Engineer, my plans are to grow this portfolio with more work done in React, Ruby and Ruby on Rails, Python, etc, which are my next objectives. Having some experience on these languages and currently working in IT, these skills will allow me to expand my frontier.
