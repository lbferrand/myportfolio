My History

Looking back at the year 2000 when I landed in this incredible country. Now my country USA. It's been a challenge and amazing ride at the same time but always grateful to be a citizen here.

Early Years

After finishing my Graphic Design studies in Mendoza, Argentina I started working as an Administration Manager for an Internet provider. I enjoyed the oportunity to learn about cable moden, internet at that time was not as popular as it is today. I was able to put my skill to work creating a system for this company that was just starting. It was a great started and learning about internet was exciting.


A couple of years after, I realized that I wanted to come to live in USA, so I started planning my move. It took me few months to organize my life. I needed to pack my old life and started again in the States. It was extremely exciting moment, although scary too. I was in my 20's at the time and I didn't speak a word of English. I didn't have family nor friends in the State either.


Present

I can believe, when I look back, and see that was 21 years ago. Happy to be here and been able to travel and speak few languages. I'm always eager to learn and constantly taking curses. That curiosity lead me to started my career as developer. I have to admit that studied on my own throw videos and free curses only confused me. I needed to find a learning project to build my foundation in development. So, I found 'SheCodes' - and here I am already as Front-End Developer.
