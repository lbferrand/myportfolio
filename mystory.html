My Story

Looking back at the year 2000 when I landed in this incredible country. Now, my country USA ❤️ . It's been a challenge and an amazing ride at the same time, but always grateful to be an american citizen.



Early Years

After finishing my Graphic Design studies in Mendoza, Argentina I started working as an Administration Manager for an Internet provider. I enjoyed the opportunity to learn about cable modem, internet at that time was not as popular as it is today. I was able to put my skill to work creating a system for this company that was just starting. It was a great beginning and learning about the internet was exciting.

A couple of years later, I realized that I wanted to come to live in USA, so I started planning my move. It took me few months to organize my life. I needed to pack my old life and start again in the States. It was an extremely exciting moment, although scary too. I was in my 20's at the time and I didn't speak a word of English. I didn't have family nor friends in the State either.


Present

I can't believe, when I look back, and see that it was 21 years ago. Happy to be here and to be able to travel and speak a few languages. I'm always eager to learn and constantly taking courses. That curiosity lead me to start a career as a developer. I must admit that study on my own through videos and free courses only confused me. I needed to find a learning process to build my foundation in development. So, I found 'SheCodes' - and here I am a Front-End and React Developer.


Future

Nobody can predict the future, but we can all work for a better one. So, I am dreaming of having an incredible career as a Developer. Increasing my knowledge to be able to work as Software Engineer, my plans are to grow this portfolio with more work done in React, Ruby and Ruby on Rails which are my next objective. Having some experience on these languages and currently working in IT, these skills will allow me to expand my frontier.
